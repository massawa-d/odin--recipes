Description

Pepperoni pizza, a beloved classic, features a simple yet satisfying combination of ingredients. A golden-brown crust, typically made with wheat flour, is spread with a rich, slightly sweet tomato sauce. Generous layers of melted mozzarella cheese, providing a creamy, cheesy texture, are then topped with slices of flavorful pepperoni, a blend of cured pork and beef seasoned with spices like paprika.
