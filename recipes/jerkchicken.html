Jerk Chicken Recipe

Home

Description

Jerk chicken, a culinary staple of Jamaica, is a flavorful and spicy dish made by marinating chicken in a blend of aromatic and pungent spices, then slow-cooking it over an open fire, often with pimento wood for a smoky flavor.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Jerk seasoning
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Add jerk seasoning to chicken and leave to marinate
  2. Add olive oil to a pan
  3. Place chicken in the pan to cook
